    About me

    Who am I?

    This is me!

    Albert Carrión Díaz (AKA Kurnic)

    I am Albert Carrión Díaz, a 23-year-old Computer Science graduate of UPC FIB (Barcelona). Luckily, I was born surrounded by video games and I grew to love them quickly. I have always been playing all kind of games and thinking about how they could be more interesting or fun. I also found I liked math and trying to do creative work, so making video games was perfect for me. I really enjoy figuring out how to create those pieces of fun and I feel I still have a long way to keep learning about it.
